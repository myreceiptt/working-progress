As you know, [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia") on August 17, 2024, declared itself as something new. [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia") is not only a social media account that actively socializes the works of **Indonesian artists** that are NFTed on various blockchains, but [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia") will also be more active in establishing cooperation, and collaboration with various existing industries, of course by continuing to actively socialize the works of **Indonesian artists** that are NFTed on various blockchains.

Some of the things that [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia") did after the declaration were to create a progressive web app and create a Discord server as a place to share various information and socialization from, by, and for all of you. We were trusted to create a progressive web app and Discord server for [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia").

The progressive web app that we created, or we can just say it as PWA, is quite simple in idea and concept. Consisting of only four main pages, namely **Home**, **About Us**, **Activities**, and **Collaborate**. The main content is articles that will be published by [**NFT Indonesia**](https://nftindonesia.xyz/ "The New NFT Indonesia"). In addition, to emphasize the simplicity of the idea and concept, we only use monochrome colors in almost all parts of the PWA.
