We are speakers and guest speakers both in the first and second **Introduction to DeFi** workshops. We create [**educational materials**](https://bace.endhonesa.com/ "Web3 Educational Materials Collection") and deliver them to the participants online. In this workshop, we do practical work on [**Plenty Network DeFi**](https://baca.endhonesa.com/tutorial-plenty-network-tezos-defi "Tutorial Plenty Network DeFi") by following the tutorial that we have created.

**Introduction to DeFi** is a small online workshop about decentralized finance on Tezos blockchain. Organized by **Tezos IDN**, the **TZAPAC** representative in **Indonesia**.
