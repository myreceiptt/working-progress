"Istiqlal Digital Legacy (IDL)" is an innovative Web3-based program from the Istiqlal Mosque to preserve Islamic cultural and artistic heritage and build a digital ecosystem. It involves the development of NFTs and blockchain technology to engage worshippers in digitally preserving Islamic culture. This program aims to bridge religious values ​​with technological advancements, making it a vital part of the Istiqlal Mosque's digitalization efforts.

What is Istiqlal Digital Legacy?

- Web3 Project: Utilizing blockchain technology, NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens), and other digital assets.
- Primary Objective: To preserve and promote Islamic cultural and artistic heritage, and to increase worshipper participation through digital platforms.
- Focus: Developing a digital ecosystem within the Istiqlal Mosque, leveraging technology for religious and cultural activities.
- IDL Activities: Istiqlal Digital Legacy (IDL) conducts NFT-related programs that enable worshippers to own a piece of Istiqlal's digital heritage.

Why Important?

- It demonstrates the Istiqlal Mosque's adaptation to the digital era, ensuring broader relevance and reach.
- Creating new resources to support mosque activities and the preservation of Islamic culture.

Overall, Istiqlal Digital Legacy is a step forward in integrating Islamic traditions with modern technology, making it one of the world's leading mosques in digital innovation.
