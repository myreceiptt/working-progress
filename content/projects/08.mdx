As the main speaker in "**Halo Ethereum Surabaya**", we create [**educational materials**](https://iqraa.straight-line.org/tutor-x-educator/ "Web3 Educational Materials Collection") and provide in-depth insights into [**the history, basic concepts, and evolution of blockchain technology**](https://iqraa.straight-line.org/tutor-x-educator/v/tutor-01-blockchain-fundamental "Materi Edukasi: Fundamental Blockchain").

"**Halo Ethereum Surabaya**" is part of **Road to Devcon Ethereum 2024**, an initiative designed to deepen knowledge about Ethereum organized by **Pintu**, a cryptocurrency trading application, and **ISTTS**, Surabaya Institute of Integrated Science and Technology.
